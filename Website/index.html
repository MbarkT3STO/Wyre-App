Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Wyre is built for speed and simplicity. Every feature is designed to get out of your way.

Instant Discovery

Devices on your network appear automatically via UDP broadcast. No pairing, no QR codes, no setup — just open the app and see who's there.

SHA-256 Verification

Every file is verified with a SHA-256 checksum after transfer. Corrupted files are caught automatically.

LAN Speed

Transfers happen directly over TCP at full local network speed. No cloud bottleneck, no upload limits.

Accept / Decline

Incoming transfers show a 30-second countdown dialog. You're always in control of what lands on your device.

Live Progress

Real-time progress bars with transfer speed in KB/s and estimated time remaining. Always know what's happening.

Dark & Light Theme

A polished UI that respects your OS preference. Switch between dark, light, or system theme at any time.

Transfer History

Full log of completed, failed, and cancelled transfers. Review what was sent and received at any time.

Cancel Anytime

Cancel transfers from either the sender or receiver side. No partial files left behind.

OS Notifications

Native system notifications when transfers complete. Works even when the app is in the background.

How it works

Three steps.
That's it.

Wyre is designed to be invisible. It just works.

01

Install Wyre

Download and install Wyre on any devices you want to connect. Available for Windows, macOS, Linux, and Android.

02

Connect to the same network

Make sure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi or LAN. Wyre automatically discovers all nearby devices — no configuration needed.

03

Drop and send

Select a device, drop your files, and hit send. The recipient gets a notification and can accept or decline. Done.
